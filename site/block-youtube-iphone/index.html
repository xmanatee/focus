Block YouTube on iPhone when it stops being useful.

YouTube can be useful in small doses and costly when it turns into a loop. Focus Blocks lets you choose a daily YouTube budget, a blocked schedule, or both.

What to set up

Daily budget

Give YouTube ten or fifteen minutes per day, then let Screen Time shield it after the limit is used.

Study or work hours

Block YouTube during deep work, class, or morning startup hours.

Schedule plus budget

Allow YouTube only inside a chosen window and still cap total use for the day.

Why Focus Blocks fits this job

Focus Blocks uses Apple's Screen Time APIs, supports websites like youtube.com and m.youtube.com, and keeps app selections private to each device. No account or tracking is required.
