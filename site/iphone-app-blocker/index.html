App blocking

An iPhone app blocker for rules you can actually keep.

Pick distracting apps or categories with Apple's Screen Time picker, then choose when they should disappear: during focus hours, outside allowed windows, or after a daily budget.

View on the App StoreRead support

Core app-blocking modes

Block during schedule

Keep distracting apps out during work, school, sleep, or morning routines.

Allow only during schedule

Make selected apps unavailable by default and only open during a chosen window.

Daily budget

Use a hard cap for apps that are fine briefly but hard to stop.

Private by default

There is no Focus Blocks account, analytics SDK, ad SDK, or backend. Rules can sync through your own iCloud account, while Screen Time app selections stay device-local.

For setup help, use Focus Blocks support to check Screen Time permission, daily budgets, synced rules, and per-device app selections.
